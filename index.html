Where do cyber security careers rank in the most in demand jobs and why should I pursue a job in cyber security?

TLDR: Why information security?

  • Job Security - Cyber security careers are currently in high demand and are projected to continue to be for the foreseeable future.
  • High Pay - The median annual wage for information security analysts is well over 100k per year.
  • Skill Up - Employers will often pay for training and development.
  • Opportunities For Advancement - High demand creates plenty of opportunities for advancement into senior roles.
  • Variety of job Roles - You can move into cloud, architecture, engineering, management, auditing or even sales. Some positions are more technical than others. Some mostly just require you to be organized or good with people.
  • Flexible Schedules and Remote Work - Security professionals enjoy a high degree of flexibility in their work, with options for remote work and flexible schedules.
  • Make a difference - Cyber security professionals play a critical role that has a huge impact on the organization.

    • "In fact, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment in the field of information security is projected to grow 35% from 2021 to 2031, which is much faster than the average for all occupations."

    -- https://www.bls.gov/ooh/computer-and-information-technology/information-security-analysts.htm
